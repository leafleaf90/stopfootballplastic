Why?

Plastic in our oceans

In just 30 years, our oceans will contain more plastic than fish (by weight). All life in our oceans are threatened. We can't be silent any longer.

How?

Being role models

Football players are idols and role models that both young and old look up and aspire to. The world of football can't keep showing the world that single-use plastic is acceptable. Something needs to change.

Action

Action

What does your club have to do to join the fight? It's easy:

  • 1. No more single-use plastic bottles at trainings or games. No more:

Join the fight and we will help arrange aluminium bottles for your club, with club and sponsor branding. We can also help arrange refilling stations at the arena and training field.

  • 2. Refill station for visitors at games. No more single-use plastic for sale in the shop. We can help you set up sales of aluminium bottles with club and sponsor branding.
